Before you begin

Care needs checklist

You can complete the checklist to see if you or someone you care about may qualify for funded social care from the council. The information you share will help you understand what help is available to support your needs, and will help you understand whether you need to pay for, or contribute to your care costs. 

At the end of the questions, we’ll give you some next steps to help you secure the care you need. You’ll also have the option to request a care needs assessment.

The form will take less than 15 minutes to complete.

Answer for the person with care needs

If you are completing this checklist for someone else, answer the questions about them and not about you.
